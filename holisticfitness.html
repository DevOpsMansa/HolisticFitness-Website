Physical Image

Physical body image is how you perceive, think and feel about your body. This can include your body's size, shape and weight, or individual body parts.
People can experience a positive or negative body image and can be influenced by both the internal and external factors in our lives.

Self image

Self image is the personal view, or mental picture, that we have of ourselves.
Self-image is an “internal dictionary” that describes the characteristics of the self, including such things as intelligent, beautiful, ugly, talented, selfish, and kind.

Spiritual Image

Pspiritual identity as a persistent sense of self that addresses ultimate questions about the nature, purpose, and meaning of life, resulting in behaviors that are consonant with the individual's core values
